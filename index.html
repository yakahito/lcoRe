Welcome to Likuyani Constituency

Generally...

Likuyani constituency is located in kakamega county and is one of the leading constituency of vital economic importance and vibrance in the western region

Bodering it to the east is the Uasin Gishu county of rift-valley and a host to the Great north road. In brief it is the gateway to western region. you can't afford to get to western and fail to notice the leading constituency. Likuyani constituency!
