Bursaries

The Likuyani constituency bursary office is keen to ensure that the largest number of genuinely needy students get access to education. Thus it has come up with a system that seeks to distribute any available funds for the education endowment of members of the constituency, from high school level to tertiary institutions.

Among the funds one can apply as bursary include the following:

  • County Bursary
  • Constituency Bursary
  • TIVET
  • EABL scholarship
  • Equity Funds
